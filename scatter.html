Meta Scatter Plot

This is a scatter plot that visualizes each hero's prevalence in the metagame by representing their winrate and pickrate in relation to each other. Popular heroes trend towards the right side, high winrate heroes trend towards the top. Less popular and unsuccessful heroes trend towards the bottom left.
